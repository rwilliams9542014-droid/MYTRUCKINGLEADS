Subscription terms

Plans are billed monthly or annually after the 3-day free trial, depending on the option selected. Cancel before your next renewal date to stop future billing. Subscription fees are fully earned once charged and are not prorated or refunded for partial billing periods, except where required by law.

Outreach compliance

MyTruckingLeads provides carrier intelligence for business prospecting. Users are responsible for following all applicable Do Not Call, TCPA, CAN-SPAM, state telemarketing, privacy, and data-use laws before calling, texting, emailing, or otherwise contacting any lead.

Frequently asked questions.

Everything agents usually want to know before requesting access.

What types of leads are included?

Plans can include new DOT leads, renewal opportunities, and carrier profile data built for commercial trucking insurance prospecting.

Can I target specific states or markets?

Yes. You can focus on the states and lead types that match your agency strategy instead of sorting through a generic national list.

How does pricing work?

Pricing is offered as flat monthly or annual access, so you can work leads without per-record fees or surprise usage charges.

Who is this built for?

MyTruckingLeads is built for insurance agents and agencies selling commercial auto, cargo, liability, and fleet coverage to trucking companies.
